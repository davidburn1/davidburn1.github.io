A detailed magnetic phase diagram of FeGe membranes with fields applied out-of-plane is determined using small-angle soft x-ray elastic scattering. In addition to the well-established skyrmion and helical phase, we identify an additional ordered phase which partly coexists with the helical phase in a pocket beneath the skyrmion phase. Furthermore, an evolution of the modulation wave vector, q, is observed when traversing the magnetic phase pocket. This study of the helical phases in thin FeGe membranes lays the foundation for their use in future magnetic storage and logic devices.
